Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 26, 2026

1RM ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our website and mobile application.

Information We Collect

When you join our waitlist, we collect your email address. We do not collect any health, fitness, or workout data at this time. If you contact us, we may also collect any information you voluntarily provide.

How We Use Your Information

We use your email address solely to notify you about the 1RM app launch and major updates. We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for their marketing purposes.

Data Storage and Security

Your information is stored securely using industry-standard practices. We use Formspree as our email form processor, which complies with applicable data protection regulations. We retain your email address until you unsubscribe or request deletion.

Cookies

Our website does not use tracking cookies or analytics services that collect personal data. We may use essential cookies required for the basic functioning of the site.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction or deletion of your data
  • Unsubscribe from communications at any time
  • Lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at privacy@1rm.app.

Third-Party Services

We use Formspree to process waitlist signup forms. Formspree's privacy policy governs their handling of your data. We recommend reviewing their privacy practices at formspree.io.

Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to individuals under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of material changes via the email address provided on the waitlist.

Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at privacy@1rm.app.
